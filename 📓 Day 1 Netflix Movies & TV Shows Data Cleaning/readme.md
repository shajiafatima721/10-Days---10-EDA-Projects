💡 Key Business Insights
📌 Netflix is shifting toward TV Shows

The analysis indicates increasing investment in TV series, reflecting changing audience binge-watching behavior.

📌 USA dominates content production

Most content originates from the United States, while several regions remain underrepresented.

📌 Drama & International genres are highly popular

This shows strong audience demand for globally relatable storytelling.

📌 Adult-focused content is highest

TV-MA and TV-14 dominate the platform, suggesting Netflix primarily targets adult and teenage audiences.

📌 Medium-duration movies are most common

Most Netflix movies fall within the 80–120 minute range.

📈 Business Recommendations
- Invest more in original TV series
- Increase multilingual and regional content
- Expand family-friendly content categories
- Focus on global audience preferences
- Refresh older low-performing content regularly
